## Your First Day Checklist

### By End of Day 1
- [ ] Set up your company email and calendar
- [ ] Join the company Slack workspace
- [ ] Complete required security training
- [ ] Meet your manager and team members

## Policies Quick Reference

### Work Hours & Location
- **Core Hours**: 10 AM - 4 PM (be available for meetings during this time)
- **Flexible Hours**: You can adjust your start/end time around core hours
- **Remote Work**: Employees can work remotely up to 2 days per week after the first month
- **Office Days**: Minimum 3 days in office for hybrid roles

### Time Off
- **Vacation**: 15 days annually (increases with tenure)
- **Sick Leave**: 10 days annually
- **Personal Days**: 3 days annually
- **How to Request**: Submit through the HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance for vacation

### Expenses
- Submit expenses through the Expense Management System
- Keep all receipts for purchases over $25
- Manager approval required for expenses over $100
- Reimbursement typically processed within 2 weeks

## Tips for Success

1. **Ask Questions**: There's no such thing as a dumb question. Everyone wants to help you succeed!
2. **Take Notes**: You'll receive a lot of information - write things down.
3. **Meet People**: Schedule coffee chats with team members to get to know them.
4. **Set Up 1:1s**: Establish regular check-ins with your manager.
5. **Be Patient**: It takes time to learn the ropes. Give yourself grace.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page, or contact IT at #it-support.

**Q: Where can I find the employee handbook?**
A: The full employee handbook is available on the HR portal under "Documents."

**Q: How do I update my personal information?**
A: Log into the HR portal and navigate to "My Profile" to update your information.

**Q: What if I'm sick and can't come in?**
A: Notify your manager as early as possible via Slack or email. Log your sick day in the HR system.

**Q: How do I book a meeting room?**
A: Use the room booking feature in your calendar app. Look for rooms with the "MTG-" prefix.
